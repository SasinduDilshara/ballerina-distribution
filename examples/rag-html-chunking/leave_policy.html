Company Leave Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025. Applies to all full-time and part-time employees.

Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per year, accruing at 1.75 days per month. Part-time employees accrue leave on a pro-rata basis.

Up to 5 unused annual leave days may be carried forward to the next calendar year. Annual leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance via the HR portal and require manager approval.

Sick Leave

All employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not accumulate from year to year.

A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days. Employees must notify their manager within 24 hours of being unable to attend work due to illness.

Casual Leave

Employees are entitled to 8 days of casual leave per year for short-term personal reasons such as family emergencies or urgent personal matters. Prior manager approval is required where possible.

Maternity and Paternity Leave

Maternity Leave: Up to 26 weeks as per applicable law. Employees must provide at least 8 weeks advance notice.

Paternity Leave: 10 days, to be taken within 3 months of the child's birth.

Bereavement Leave

Up to 5 working days for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, or sibling). Documentation may be required.

Leave Without Pay

Leave without pay may be approved for personal emergencies or extended personal needs. Both manager and HR approval is required. It does not affect job status but may impact benefits depending on duration.

Leave Request Procedure

Submit leave requests via the HR portal or by email to your manager. Include the type of leave, requested dates, and reason where required. The manager will respond within 2 business days. HR will update your leave balance accordingly.

For questions, contact HR at hr@sample.com.

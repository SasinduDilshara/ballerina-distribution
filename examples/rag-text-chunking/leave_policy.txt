Company Leave Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Applies to: All full-time and part-time employees

1. Annual Leave (Paid Time Off)

All full-time employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per year, accruing at 1.75 days per month. Part-time employees accrue leave on a pro-rata basis.

Unused annual leave: Up to 5 days may be carried forward to the next calendar year. Any remaining unused days will be forfeited or may be encashed at the end of the year for employees who have completed 12 months of continuous service.

Annual leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance via the HR portal and require manager approval.

2. Sick Leave

All employees (full-time and part-time) are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not accumulate from year to year.

A medical certificate is required for sick leave absences longer than 2 consecutive days. Employees must notify their manager within 24 hours of being unable to attend work due to illness.

3. Casual Leave

Employees are entitled to 8 days of casual leave per year for short-term personal reasons such as family emergencies, personal appointments, or other urgent matters. Prior manager approval is required where possible.

4. Maternity and Paternity Leave

Maternity Leave: Up to 26 weeks as per applicable law. Employees must provide at least 8 weeks' advance notice.

Paternity Leave: 10 days, to be taken within 3 months of the child's birth.

5. Bereavement Leave

Up to 5 working days for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). Documentation may be required.

6. Leave Without Pay

Leave without pay may be approved on a case-by-case basis for personal emergencies or extended personal needs. Approval from both the manager and HR is required. Leave without pay does not affect job status but may impact benefits depending on duration.

7. Leave Request Procedure

Submit leave requests via the HR portal or by email to your manager. Include the type of leave, requested dates, and reason (where required). The manager will approve or reject within 2 business days. HR will update your leave balance accordingly.

For questions, contact HR at hr@sample.com.
